# Windrose Compass

**Live site:** [https://windrosegame.net](https://windrosegame.net)

Windrose Compass is an **unofficial, fan-run companion** for [**Windrose**](https://playwindrose.com/)—a pirate survival adventure in **Early Access** on Steam. This README is written for players and readers: what the game is, what you will find on the site, and how to use it. It is **not** affiliated with the Windrose developers or publishers; always treat in-game text and official channels as the source of truth after patches.

---

## About Windrose (the game)

Windrose drops you into a **persistent archipelago** where you sail between islands, build camps, craft gear, and fight **PvE-focused** threats alone or with friends. Progression blends **exploration, survival loops, and souls-inspired melee combat**, with ships and naval encounters woven into the fantasy of life on the waves. The developers pitch it as an adventure you can play **solo or in co-op**, with online sessions built around hosts, invite codes, and (optionally) dedicated servers for crews in different time zones.

---

## What makes it stand out

- **Open-world sailing** with meaningful ship progression, wharf upgrades, and loadout decisions that matter in fights and long voyages.
- **Co-op friendly design**: share a world, split roles (gathering, crafting, navigation), and keep everyone on the **same client version** after updates.
- **Deep crafting and camp systems**: resources, consumables, food buffs, and workshop tiers that reward planning—not just grinding random drops.
- **Combat with weight**: stamina, weapon families, talents, and gear choices combine into distinct builds rather than one universal “best sword.”
- **Early Access honesty**: names, balance, and UI strings can shift week to week—community wikis and maps exist to help you **verify** details in the same session you play.

## Guides on Windrose Compass

Long-form **editorial guides** cover routes, builds, gathering metas, ships, and multiplayer workflow. Each guide is a full article (not a stub). On the guides hub you can **filter by topic**—for example gameplay systems, fishing, ships, or gear and builds—while the list stays sorted by **newest publish date** first.

Individual guide URLs look like:

`https://windrosegame.net/guides/<article-slug>`

Use the hub to discover slugs and to jump between related wiki or map pages linked inside the prose.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is this site official?**  
No. Windrose Compass is a fan project. Official news, support, and purchases belong to the developers and storefronts (e.g. Steam, [playwindrose.com](https://playwindrose.com/)).

**Why does my build or item name not match an article?**  
Early Access patches can rename or rebalance content faster than prose updates. Cross-check the **wiki tables** and your in-game tooltips the same day you read a guide.

**Can I use the map offline or in-game?**  
The interactive map runs in your browser on **windrosegame.net**. It is a planning aid; it does not replace the in-game map.

**Who do I contact for game bugs or account issues?**  
Use **official** Windrose / Steam / publisher support. The site’s legal pages explain how **site feedback** differs from game support.

**Are mods safe or endorsed?**  
Mod articles are **spotlights and notes** only. Read each mod’s own page on Nexus or the author’s page, back up saves, and only install what you trust.
